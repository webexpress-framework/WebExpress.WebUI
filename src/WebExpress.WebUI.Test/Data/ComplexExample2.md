# Princess Peach

Princess Peach is the graceful ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom. Known for her kindness, courage, and signature pink dress, she often joins Mario and friends on heroic adventures.

## Roles & Abilities

- Royal leadership
- Magical powers
- Skilled in sports and kart racing
- Star of her own game: *Princess Peach: Showtime!*
