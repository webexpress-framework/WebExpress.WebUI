# Bowser

Bowser, also known as King Koopa, is the main antagonist of the Super Mario series. He’s known for his spiked shell, fire breath, and relentless attempts to kidnap Princess Peach.

## Characteristics

- Species: Koopa
- Signature Move: Fire breath
- Role: Main villain in most Mario games
- Allies: Koopalings, Bowser Jr.
