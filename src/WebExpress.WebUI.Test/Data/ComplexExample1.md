# Super Mario

Super Mario is the iconic hero of the Mushroom Kingdom. He wears blue overalls, a red cap, and is known for his jumping ability and sense of adventure.

## Abilities

- Jumping
- Using power-ups
- Defeating enemies
- Rescuing Princess Peach
